ART

Complex Fractals

Mathematics contain immense beauty and elegance in terms of symmetry. This is my attempt to reveil this beauty through the visual sense. To do this, complex functions are iterated until a a reasonable divergence developes between bound and unbound results. Finally, this gradient gets colorized producing the pattern you see. This technique of producing fractals is the same method used in imaging the Mandelbrot set.
